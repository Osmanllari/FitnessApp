Abs Workouts


Sit-Ups
Sit-ups are a classic abdominal exercise that use your body weight to strengthen and tone the core-stabilizing abdominal muscles.

Spiderman Plank
The spiderman plank is a bodyweight exercise that works mainly the core and the upper body.

Plank
The plank is a bodyweight exercise which involves holding the trunk part of your body in a straight line off the ground.

Abs Roll-out
The ab rollout is an exercise designed to target the core muscles, including the rectus abdominis, obliques, and erector spinae.

Leg Raise
The leg raise is a strength training exercise which targets the iliopsoas and are also often used to strengthen the rectus abdominis muscle, the internal and external oblique muscles.

Reverse Crunch
The reverse crunch is an intermediate level variation of the popular abdominal crunch exercise. It exercises the full length of the deep lower abs.
